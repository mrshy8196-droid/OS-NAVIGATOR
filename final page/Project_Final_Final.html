Our Group's Adventures in Building This Project:

Special Dedication & Appreciation

Who We Are:

We are students from the Faculty of Computers and Artificial Intelligence, Al-Azhar University in Cairo (Computer Science Department). To bring this project to life with the highest quality, we organized ourselves into a structured system consisting of five specialized groups. Each group had a dedicated leader, all coordinated by a "Leaders Group" that supervised the entire workflow.

Our Vision:

Our main goal was to create a comprehensive summary of the Operating Systems (OS) curriculum. This project was uniquely developed using Batch files (.bat) and HTML to provide a smooth and interactive experience.
